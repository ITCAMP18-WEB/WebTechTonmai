Take Care / Character

Watering
  • Do not need frequent watering. Monstera loves water and moisture. But choose pots and soil that drains well. to prevent waterlogging Keep an eye on it by poking your finger into the soil. If the soil begins to dry 50-70%, then water more, approximately 2-3 times a day.
